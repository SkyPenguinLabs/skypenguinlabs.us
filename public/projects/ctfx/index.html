CTFx

A CTF and lab operations surface for running focused security exercises without turning the site into a course storefront.

Project Facts

StatusProject brief
MaturityEarly buildout
AudienceTeams preparing exercises, demos, and security training infrastructure.

Problem

Practice environments need structure, repeatability, and evidence capture while staying lightweight enough to run in public or private labs.

Approach

CTFx keeps challenge operations, lab metadata, and participant-facing surfaces separated from commercial course delivery.
